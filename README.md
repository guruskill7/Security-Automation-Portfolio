## Projects

### 1. Security Alert Triage Assistant
**Problem:** SOC teams are overwhelmed with 1000+ daily alerts, leading to alert fatigue and missed critical threats.

**Solution:** Automated triage system that prioritizes alerts based on:
- Asset criticality
- Threat intelligence indicators
- Event severity and frequency

**Business Impact:**
- Reduces triage time by 60%
- Ensures critical alerts are addressed first
- Prevents analyst burnout

**Problem:** Executives don't understand technical vulnerability reports (CVE numbers, CVSS scores) and can't make informed risk decisions.

**Solution:** Converts technical scan data into executive-friendly reports with:
- Business impact analysis
- Cost/risk estimates
- Prioritized recommendations
- Timeline and effort estimates

**Business Impact:**
- Speeds up patch approval process
